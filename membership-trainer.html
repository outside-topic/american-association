The Membership Process

Becoming an AAC certified professional is a structured yet straightforward process designed to uphold global standards.

01

Online Application

Fill out our comprehensive online form with your personal and professional details.

02

Document Review

Our ethics board reviews your instructional experience and academic documentation.

03

Verification

Final verification of your credentials against our global competency framework.

04

Certification

Receive your global designation, digital badge, and welcome kit.

Available Tiers

Trainer Designation Categories

MOST POPULAR

Professional Trainer (PT)

Designed for mid-career professionals with established instructional expertise.

  • 5+ Years Experience
  • Advanced Instruction
  • Global Network Access
  • Digital Professional Badge

Fellow Trainer (FT)

The highest level of recognition for elite instructional leaders globally.

  • 10+ Years Experience
  • Strategic Leadership
  • Mentorship Opportunities
  • Fellow Digital Registry

Associate Trainer (AT)

Perfect for emerging professionals building their instructional career.

  • 0-3 Years Experience
  • Core Pedagogy Training
  • Community Support
  • Associate Member Badge
