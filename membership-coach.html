Elite Designations

Coach Accreditation Tiers

MOST RECOMMENDED

Professional Coach (PC)

Ideally suited for practicing coaches with established client success records.

  • 500+ Coaching Hours
  • Advanced Methodology
  • International Directory
  • Member Discounts

Fellow Coach (FC)

A prestigious designation for transformative leaders in the coaching industry.

  • 2500+ Coaching Hours
  • Industry Leadership
  • Specialized Mentorship
  • FC Global Accreditation

Associate Coach (AC)

Designed for dedicated coaches starting their professional journey.

  • 100+ Coaching Hours
  • Foundational Competencies
  • Peer Support Access
  • ASTC Membership Portal
